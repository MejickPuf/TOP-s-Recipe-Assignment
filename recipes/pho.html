Pho


Description

Phở or pho is a Vietnamese soup dish consisting of broth, rice noodles (bánh phở), herbs, and meat (usually beef (phở bò), sometimes chicken (phở gà)). Pho is a popular food in Vietnam where it is served in households, street stalls and restaurants countrywide. Pho is considered Vietnam's national dish.

Ingredients

  • Rice noodles
  • Beef
  • Herbs
  • Chili sauce and Hoisin
  • Beef bones
  • Spice bag
  • Seasoning

Steps

  1. Wash your beef bones then add to a pot with water.
  2. Boil bones, add in your spice bag and seasoning, then reduce to a simmer for 8 hours.
  3. Slice raw beef into thin strips and prepare your garnishes - herbs and condiments.
  4. Add rice noodles to a bowl along with raw beef. Ladle in your broth and garnish with your herbs and condiments.
